Privacy Policy for NDAC App


Effective Date: March 03, 2026



North Deering Alliance Church (NDAC), based in Portland, Maine, operates the NDAC mobile application.



We are committed to protecting your privacy.



Information We Collect


The NDAC app does not require user accounts and does not collect personal information such as names, emails, or phone numbers.



Prayer requests submitted through the app are private and not publicly displayed.



The app may collect a device identifier token for the purpose of delivering push notifications. This information is used solely to send church updates and announcements.



How We Use Information


Information collected is used only to:

Deliver push notifications

Improve app functionality



We do not sell, rent, or share user data with third parties.



Data Security


All data transmission is encrypted using secure HTTPS protocols.



Third-Party Services


The app uses Firebase Cloud Messaging to send notifications. Firebase may process device identifiers in accordance with their privacy policy.
