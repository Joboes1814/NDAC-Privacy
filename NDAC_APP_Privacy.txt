The NDAC App is developed for the North Deering Alliance Church in Portland to help share sermons, events, announcements, and prayer requests with the church community.

Information We Collect

The NDAC App does not require users to create an account and does not collect personal information such as names, email addresses, or phone numbers.

Users may voluntarily submit prayer requests through the application. These messages are intended solely for prayer support within the church community.

Prayer Request Handling

Prayer requests submitted through the application are processed temporarily. These requests are not permanently stored and are deleted after being read.

Data Sharing

The NDAC App does not sell, share, or distribute user data to third parties.

Security

We take reasonable steps to ensure that submitted information is handled securely.Contact Us
